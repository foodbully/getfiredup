# Canadian Retirement Calculator (Rev 0)
This calculator will determine if your current and future savings will be enough for you to retire.

## Inputs

### Timeline
- Current Age
- Retire Age
- Death Age

### Goals
- Annual Expenses
- Medical Expenses
- Estate Value

### Current Savings
- RRSP Balance
- TFSA Balance
- Non-Reg Balance
- Non-Reg Adjust Cost Basis (ACB)

### Investment Rates
- Return Rate
- Dividend Rate

### Pension Sources
- Pension Income
- Pension Age
- CPP Income
- CPP Age
- OAS Income
- OAS Age


## Assumptions
- RRSP will be converted to RRIF upon retirement
- Retirement savings drawdown order will be: (1) RRSP, (2) Non-Reg, and (3) TFSA
- During retirement, maxing out the TFSA will be funded by the dividends
- Savings contribution order will be: (1) RRSP, (2) TFSA, and (3) Non-Reg


## Tax Credits
- Dividend (eligible)
- Pension Income 
- Age Amount
- Medical Expenses
